Games - All the game modes we offer

FFA

If you are one of those PvP addicted ones, do not fear to jump down in the no going back Free For All arena to compete against all the other players on the network. Use your best strategies and kits to become the most godlike warrior.

Faction

Gather your friends and create a faction, compete against other factions by finding their hidden base and blow it up. Become the most powerful one on the server and have a chance to win the honorable prize at the end of the season.

ArenaPvP

Sign yourself up to a queue and be prepared to fight another player in the numerous of maps to choose from. Practice your battle skills and become the top 1vs1 warrior across the entire network.

UHC

Along with lots of other player, get thrown into an open world with a border miles away. Gather resources underground and become the most powerful warrior in the world to be the last one standing and win but be aware, natural regeneration is off.
